# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## TL;DR

- Be respectful. Disagree on technical merits, not on people.
- No personal attacks, no spam, no off-topic political posts in issues or PRs.
- Maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, or PRs that violate the Covenant.

## Reporting

Email **roniesds@gmail.com** with subject `cli2mcp conduct`. Reports are handled privately.
